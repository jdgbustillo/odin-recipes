Como hacer un rico pollo agridulce

Indulge in a symphony of bold flavors and irresistible textures with our sensational General Tso's Chicken recipe. Picture tender chunks of succulent chicken coated in a tantalizing blend of sweet, spicy, and tangy sauce, creating a mouthwatering harmony that will transport your taste buds to culinary bliss. Crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside, this beloved Chinese-American classic is a culinary masterpiece that will leave you craving more. Prepare to embark on a culinary adventure that promises to tantalize your senses and elevate your home cooking to extraordinary levels.

Ingredients

Chicken:

  1. 4 cups vegetable oil for frying
  2. 1 large egg
  3. 1 ½ pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces
  4. 1 teaspoon salt
  5. 1 teaspoon white sugar
  6. 1 pinch white pepper
  7. 1 cup cornstarch

Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons chopped green onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 6 dried whole red chilies
  • 1 strip orange zest
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons peanut oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • ¼ teaspoon ground ginger
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • ¼ cup water
